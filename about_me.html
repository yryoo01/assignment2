My Memorable Trip

In 2022, my family and I drove all the way from Rhode Island to California. We stopped by many destinations to travel, including New York, Washington D.C., the Grand Canyon, and Las Vegas. Not only was it fun to stop by all the fun places, but it was also nice to see scenery that we have never seen before during the drive. The entire trip lasted for 2 weeks and was one of the most memorable times in my life. We only drove so far because we were moving, but it was more of a fun traveling experience than a long and boring drive.

My Favorite Restaurant

The Interstellar BBQ - This restaurant is my favorite because they have very unique flavoring for the BBQ and the meat is very tender and juicy. It was the best Texan BBQ I've ever had. It's a little pricy and out of the way, but if I got an opportunity to have it again I would be super excited.

My Favorite Books

Book Name Cover Image Author Summary
The Night Circus Erin Morgenstern This book feels like studying abroad but in a dream. Two magicians are forced into this weird competition, but instead of fighting, they flirt through magical architecture. The plot wanders, but the vibes carry it.
Rebecca Daphne du Maurier It's about a girl who marries a rich guy way too fast and then moves into his mansion, where everyone is still obsessed with his dead wife. The whole thing feels like imposter syndrome turned into a gothic novel, and it's weirdly relatable.
Dracula Bram Stoker Victorian people write extremely long letters about a vampire. It's part horror and part travel diary, but it's fun once you get into the rhythm.
The Haunting of Hill House Shirley Jackson A group of strangers stays in a haunted house for "research," which goes about as well as you'd expect. The house gaslights everyone, and the main character slowly unravels in a way that feels like a semester's worth of stress condensed into a weekend.
